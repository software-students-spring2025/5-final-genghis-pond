About Us
For animal lovers around the world
We believe in the preservation of all species by means of observation and algorithmic recognition. Our goal is to educate others and foster a community of biology enthusiasts using modern technology.

At Genghis Pond, we are deeply committed to ethical practices in the development and use of our animal identification technology. We believe that technology should be used to promote education, conservation, and responsible wildlife engagement.

Our app tracks biodiversity, observes migration patterns and population changes, and helps document rare or endangered species.

All images processed by our platform are treated with strict respect for privacy and intellectual property rights.
